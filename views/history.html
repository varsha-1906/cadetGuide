History Syllabus for Defence Exams

Ancient India

  • Indus Valley Civilization (2500-1500 BCE)
  • Vedic Period, Mauryan Empire (321-185 BCE)
  • Gupta Empire (320-550 CE), Post-Gupta Period
  • South Indian Kingdoms: Cholas, Cheras, Pandyas

Medieval India

  • Delhi Sultanate (1206-1526): Slave, Khilji, Tughlaq, Lodhi
  • Mughal Empire (1526-1857): Babur to Aurangzeb
  • Vijayanagara Empire, Maratha Empire
  • Bhakti and Sufi Movements

Modern India

  • Advent of Europeans, British Rule in India
  • Revolt of 1857, Freedom Struggle (1885-1947)
  • Indian National Congress, Partition of Bengal
  • Gandhian Era, Partition and Independence

World History

  • French Revolution (1789), American Revolution
  • World War I (1914-18), World War II (1939-45)
  • Industrial Revolution, Russian Revolution
  • Cold War, Decolonization
