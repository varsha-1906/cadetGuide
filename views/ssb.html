SSB Interview Eligibility

  • Must have cleared the written examination (NDA/CDS/AFCAT/etc.)
  • Must meet medical standards as per respective service requirements
  • Age: As per the written exam eligibility (typically 16.5-24 years)
  • No specific academic requirements for SSB, but must meet entry exam criteria
  • Physical fitness: Good overall health, no disqualifying medical conditions
  • Must carry all original documents and call letter on interview day

SSB Interview Syllabus

Stage I: Screening Test (Day 1)

  • Intelligence Test (Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning)
  • Picture Perception & Discussion Test (PPDT)
  • Only qualified candidates proceed to Stage II

Stage II: Testing (Day 2-5)

  • Psychology Tests: TAT, WAT, SRT, SD
  • GTO Tasks: GD, GPE, PGT, HGT, IOR, CT, FGT, Lecturette, Command Task
  • Personal Interview: Detailed interview by IO
  • Conference: Final board conference

üìò SSB Interview Pattern

  • Day 1 (Screening): Intelligence Test + PPDT - Duration: ~2 hours
  • Day 2 (Psychology): TAT (12 pictures), WAT (60 words), SRT (60 situations), SD (4 topics) - Duration: ~4-5 hours
  • Day 3-4 (GTO): Group tasks, Individual tasks, Command Task - Duration: Full day
  • Day 5 (Interview & Conference): Personal Interview (45-60 min), Final Conference - Duration: Full day
  • Total Duration: 5 days
  • Result: Announced on Day 5 evening

‚úÖ Note: SSB interview assesses Officer Like Qualities (OLQs): Planning & Organizing, Social Adjustment, Sense of Responsibility, Initiative, Self-Confidence, Speed of Decision, Ability to Influence, Liveliness, Determination, Courage, Stamina, and Group Effectiveness.
