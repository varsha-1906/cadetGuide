Physical Fitness Components

Cardiovascular Fitness

  • Running: 1.6 km in 6-7 minutes
  • Cycling: 30-45 minutes moderate pace
  • Swimming: 20-30 minutes continuous
  • Jump Rope: 10-15 minutes intervals
  • Benefits: Improves heart health, stamina, lung capacity

Strength Training

  • Push-ups: 10-15 in one minute
  • Pull-ups/Chin-ups: 3-6 repetitions
  • Sit-ups/Crunches: 20-25 in one minute
  • Weight Training: Progressive resistance
  • Benefits: Builds muscle, improves bone density

Flexibility & Mobility

  • Stretching: Daily 15-20 minutes
  • Yoga: Improves flexibility and balance
  • Dynamic Warm-up: Before exercise
  • Static Stretching: After exercise
  • Benefits: Prevents injuries, improves range of motion

Endurance Building

  • Long Distance Running: 3-5 km
  • Circuit Training: Multiple exercises
  • Interval Training: High-low intensity
  • Cross Training: Variety of activities
  • Benefits: Sustained performance, better recovery
