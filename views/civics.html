Civics & Polity Syllabus for Defence Exams

Indian Constitution

  • Preamble, Salient Features, Fundamental Rights (Articles 12-35)
  • Directive Principles of State Policy (Articles 36-51)
  • Fundamental Duties (Article 51A)
  • Constitutional Amendments (Important ones)
  • Schedules of the Constitution (12 Schedules)
  • Parts of the Constitution (25 Parts)

Union Government

  • President: Powers, Functions, Election Process
  • Prime Minister & Council of Ministers
  • Parliament: Lok Sabha, Rajya Sabha, Sessions
  • Supreme Court: Structure, Jurisdiction, Powers
  • Judiciary: Appointment, Removal, Functions

State Government

  • Governor: Powers, Functions, Appointment
  • Chief Minister & State Council of Ministers
  • State Legislature: Vidhan Sabha, Vidhan Parishad
  • High Court: Structure, Jurisdiction, Powers
  • Local Government: Panchayati Raj, Municipalities

Constitutional & Statutory Bodies

  • Election Commission: Functions, Powers
  • UPSC, SPSC: Composition, Functions
  • CAG (Comptroller & Auditor General)
  • Finance Commission, NITI Aayog
  • National Human Rights Commission (NHRC)
