Syllabus

Mathematics

  • Arithmetic: Number system, HCF, LCM, profit and loss, percentage, ratio, and average
  • Algebra: Basic operations and quadratic equations
  • Geometry: Lines, angles, triangles, and circles
  • Mensuration: Area, perimeter, and volume
  • Trigonometry: Basics (especially for technical roles)

General Knowledge

  • Indian Constitution and history: Including the Indian freedom struggle
  • Geography: Physical and political geography, including solar system, rivers, and countries and capitals
  • Current events: Important national and international events, awards, and sports
  • Defense and general facts: Including abbreviations, major institutions, and significant days

General Science

  • Physics: Nature of matter, laws of motion, work, energy, power, and electricity
  • Chemistry: Basic reactions, the periodic table, acids, bases, salts, and carbon and its compounds
  • Biology: Human body basics, diseases, plants, animals, cells, and tissues

Reasoning

  • Series completion and coding-decoding
  • Puzzles, blood relations, and direction sense
  • Analogies and logical Venn diagrams
  • Alphabet and number-based sequences

Reasoning

  • Grammar: Including prepositions and sentence structure
  • Vocabulary
  • Comprehension
  • Sentence completion
